Miso Soup Recipe

Description

This is a description of a miso soup

I love drinking miso soup

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Dashi granules
  • Miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Green onions

Steps:

  1. Bring the water and dashi grnules to a boil
  2. Reduce the heat and whisk in miso paste
  3. Stir in the tofu and green onions
  4. Simmer and serve
